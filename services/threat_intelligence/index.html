Actionable

SYN engages with your key stakeholders to create or mature the collection, analysis and dissemination of threat intelligence related to relevant threat actors and probable threat scenarios to your organization. The service can also enable your organization to develop or mature its threat intelligence program, enhance your security operations and help you define an organization-specific cyber threat landscape tailored to your unique business environment. We do not disappoint.

Holistic

Modern cyber attackers are sophisticated, well-funded, well-organized and use highly-targeted techniques that leave technology-only security strategies exposed. To identify and stop attackers, organizations need to understand how they think, how they work, and what they want. But most organizations rely solely on legacy, signature-based intelligence feeds that provide a false sense of safety. Those types of intelligence cannot help anticipate attacks or guide responses. SYN combines outward-looking adversary intelligence with best-of-breed breach victim and machine-based intelligence for a holistic view of proliferating threats before, during and after attacks.

Intelligence in action across the entire organization

SYN Threat Intelligence provides organizations complete, actionable threat intelligence across all categories of threats. Strategically, threat intelligence provides a true risk profile, empowering security leaders to devise strategies around changing threat conditions and secure the resources to defend against them. Operationally, intelligence prioritizes alerts, allowing security teams to respond to the risks that matter most by qualifying the thousands of alerts generated from technology-only strategies. Tactically, intelligence is a force multiplier, aiding in the identification, analysis and remediation of threats inside and at the perimeter of network walls. Cyber threat intelligence provides valuable insight for use throughout the organization, from augmenting alerts to discussing risk with the board.
